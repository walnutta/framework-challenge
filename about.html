About CloneLab

CloneLab provides secure, automated cloning workflows designed for laboratories and educational environments. Our mission is to make biological cloning processes safer, more transparent, and reproducible.

Our Mission

We aim to simplify and safeguard lab operations through automation and standardized digital workflows. CloneLab ensures compliance with biosafety standards and enables data integrity through detailed tracking.

Our Team

CloneLab is built by a team of engineers, scientists, and educators passionate about safe biotechnology innovation. We collaborate closely with research labs to continuously improve usability and reliability.
